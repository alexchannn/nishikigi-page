About Me

I'm a girl who puts on a brave and cheerful front, but who has a heart that can break easily. I believe in fairness in the world and I always want to know the truth.
I believe in justice and the strength of honesty, kindness, and love. I hope one day I can help make the world a better place, but I know it's a tall order and might require a lot of hard work and determination.
